What is the goal of Viralize?

Viralize is a social media analytics platform designed for Palomar Health. Its goal is to help visualize and analyze social media performance across platforms like Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn. By offering real-time insights into post reach, engagement, and content types, Viralize empowers healthcare professionals to spread health education more effectively.
